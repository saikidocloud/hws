AWS SageMaker ML Hands‑on Bootcamp (9 Sessions)

This practical hands‑on course is designed for anyone starting out with machine learning and wanting to experience model development and deployment on AWS. Using AWS SageMaker, you’ll go through model building, inference, and MLOps in this intensive bootcamp. It is easy to follow—especially for cloud and ML beginners—and emphasizes step‑by‑step learning through doing.

🔶 Overview

  • Understand machine learning fundamentals and approaches
  • Practice data preprocessing and model training
  • Develop using SageMaker Studio Lab / Notebooks
  • Learn deployment and API‑ization
  • Explore automated MLOps processes

🔶 Instructor Introduction

An AWS‑certified instructor will lead the program. They hold the AWS Authorized Instructor credential and have served as a part‑time IT lecturer at university. With extensive project experience in system design, development, infrastructure buildout, and cloud migrations at major financial and engineering companies in Tokyo, they specialize in bridging beginners to real‑world practical levels through hands‑on training delivered nationwide.
